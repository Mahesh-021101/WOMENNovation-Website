TIMELINE

Phase I (Feb 12 - Feb 22, 2023)

Project Proposal

Student teams must select one of the four available project tracks and submit an idea related to it, including a functional and basic technical design using the provided template

Phase II (Feb 25 - March 5, 2023)

Prototype Development

Shortlisted teams will move on to building their prototype, which must include a complete prototype, design, final presentation, and code to be submitted at the end of this round

Phase III (March 8, 2023)

Final Presentation Deck

Shortlisted student teams will get 10 minutes to present followed by 5 minutes of Q&A to present their prototype to the judging panel
