PROJECT TRACKS

Innovate For Society

This track focuses on creating solutions that address societal issues faced by women. Examples include but not limited to, creating solutions for gender equality, and addressing the gender pay gap. Participants in this track will be challenged to come up with innovative ideas that can make a real impact on women's lives and contribute to a more equitable and inclusive society

Innovate For Healthy Living

This track focuses on creating solutions that improve the physical and mental well-being of women. Examples include but not limited to, developing technologies to improve maternal health, mental health, and creating fitness programs. Participants in this track will be encouraged to think creatively and come up with ideas that promote healthy living for women

Innovate For Security

This track focuses on creating solutions that enhance the safety and security of women. Examples include but not limited to, developing personal safety apps, designing home security systems, and creating transportation solutions. Participants in this track will be challenged to think about how technology can be used to protect women and empower them to feel safe and secure

Innovate For Education

This track focuses on creating solutions that improve the education and career opportunities for women. Examples include but not limited to, developing online education platforms, creating mentorship programs, and designing initiatives to promote gender diversity in the workplace. Participants in this track will be encouraged to think about how technology can be used to bridge the gender gap in education and open up new opportunities for women
